Volunteer with us!

Thank you for your interest in joining our Team!.

We appreciate every effort you make.

We are looking for volunteers who want to make a difference in the lives of abandoned and orphaned animals in our care. We need friendly, outgoing and personable volunteers to assist the public and work with the staff in our animal shelters and at on-site and off-site adoption events.

Volunteer opportunities are available during our business hours. Every day from 8:00 am to 5:00 pm. Shelters are closed on holidays.

What a volunteer typically would do.

  • Schedule return visit with adopter once a month for the first year.
  • Answer incoming calls and emails regarding to the adoption and donations.
  • Keep company with kittens and cultivate their physical and mental health.
